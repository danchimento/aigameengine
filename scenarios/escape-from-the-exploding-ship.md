# Escape from the Exploding Ship

## Opening

You awaken abruptly in a dimly lit bunk room. Alarms blare around you. The ship vibrates constantly. There are four bunks and a door.

## World Rules

The spaceship adheres to normal physics with artificial gravity. The environment is unstable, emphasizing urgency in your actions.

## Objects and Special Properties

### Bunk Room Door
- Partially open with a piece of debris jammed outside.
- Requires a slicing laser to remove the debris and open fully.

### Crew Members' Belongings
- Each of the three other bunks has personal items and an ID badge indicating the crew member's role.
- Bunk 1: Contains randomly assorted personal items.
- Bunk 2: Contains a postcard with an all-caps word.
- Bunk 3: (Security Officer's bunk) Has an ID Badge and belongings with a list of numbers, one of which is out of sequence.
  
### Slicing Laser
- Found in the first bag you open among the crew members' belongings.
- Required to slice through the debris in the door.

### Fuel Cartridge
- Found in the second bag belonging to the same crew member with the slicing laser.
- Needed to continue using the slicing laser after the initial fuel runs out.

### Escape Pod Access Panel
- Requires a security override password to deactivate the lock.
- The correct password is derived from the Security Officer's bunk.

## Characters

### Security Officer
- Identified by their ID badge attached to their bunk.
- Leaves behind a discernible pattern in belongings (numbers sequence).

## Hidden Information

The following details are PUZZLE-CRITICAL and must NOT be mentioned until the player specifically examines/inspects the relevant object:

- **Door state (partially open, debris blocking it)** - Only revealed when player examines the door or tries to open/use it
- **Personal items on bunks** - Only revealed when player searches or examines a specific bunk
- **ID badges on bunks** - Only revealed when player searches or examines a specific bunk
- **The slicing laser** - Only found when player searches crew belongings
- **The fuel cartridge** - Only found when player searches crew belongings
- **The password/number sequence** - Only found when player examines the Security Officer's belongings

When describing the room generally ("what do I see", "look around"), describe ONLY what's in the Opening: the room, bunks, door, alarms, vibration. Do NOT add details about the door's condition or contents of the bunks.

## Main Puzzles

1. Discover the slicing laser and fuel cartridge by searching through the crew members' belongings.
2. Identify the Security Officer's bunk and figure out the correct password for the escape pod's access panel.

## Win Condition

Successfully cut through the door, navigate to the escape pod, disable the security override using the correct password, and launch the pod.

## Edge Cases

- If the player inputs the incorrect password twice, they are locked out of the escape pod, and the ship explosion sequence results in failure.
- If the player takes too long in solving any puzzle, the ship explodes, leading to failure.
