# The Room

## Opening

You wake up in a small room with no memory of how you got here...

## World Rules

This world operates under normal physical rules. Objects behave as they would in reality unless otherwise specified below.

## Objects and Special Properties

### Door
- Locked (requires the key to unlock)
- When unlocked and opened: You win! You've escaped the room.

### Box
- Contains a key
- Closed at start

### Key
- Inside the box
- Unlocks the door

### Light Switch
- Currently in the up position (lights on)
- Controls the room lights

### Chair
- A simple wooden chair

## Win Condition

Unlock and open the door.
